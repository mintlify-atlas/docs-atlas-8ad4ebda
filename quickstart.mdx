---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Get from zero to tracking your first vehicle in under 5 minutes."
---

Follow these steps to create your account, add a vehicle, and log your first trip on KilomeTracker.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [kilometracker.vercel.app/register](https://kilometracker.vercel.app/register) and fill out the registration form:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `username` | Your display name |
    | `email` | Your email address |
    | `password` | Choose a strong password |
    | `confirmPassword` | Re-enter your password |

    Click **Register** to create your account. You'll be redirected to the login page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in">
    Enter your email and password on the login page. After a successful login, you're redirected to the vehicle dashboard.

    <Note>
      If you were invited by an admin, your account may already be created. Use the email and password provided to you.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first vehicle">
    From the dashboard, click **Add Vehicle** and fill out the form:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `alias` | A short, unique identifier for the vehicle (e.g., `my-car`, `truck-01`). Used in all URLs — **cannot be changed later**. |
    | `marca` | Vehicle brand/make (e.g., Toyota, Honda) |
    | `modelo` | Model year (e.g., 2021) |
    | `plates` | License plate number |
    | `kilometrajeInicial` | Current odometer reading in kilometers |

    Click **Save**. Your vehicle appears on the dashboard.

    <Tip>
      Choose an alias that is short, lowercase, and memorable. It becomes part of the URL for all vehicle-related pages (e.g., `/vehicle-stats/my-car`).
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log your first route">
    Navigate to **Add Route** in the sidebar and fill out the form:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `vehicleAlias` | Select your vehicle |
    | `distanciaRecorrida` | Distance traveled in kilometers |
    | `fecha` | Date of the trip |
    | `notasAdicionales` | Optional notes about the trip |

    Click **Save**. KilomeTracker immediately updates the vehicle's total mileage (`kilometrajeTotal`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log your first refuel">
    Navigate to **Add Refuel** and fill out the form:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `vehicleAlias` | Select your vehicle |
    | `tipoCombustible` | Fuel type: `Regular`, `Premium`, `Diesel`, `Eléctrico`, `Híbrido`, or `V-Power` |
    | `cantidadGastada` | Total amount spent on fuel |
    | `galones` | Number of gallons filled (optional, required for efficiency calculations) |
    | `fecha` | Date of the refuel |
    | `notasAdicionales` | Optional notes |

    Click **Save**. Once you have enough refuels logged, visit the fuel analysis page to see your km/liter and km/gallon efficiency metrics.

    <Info>
      Gallons (`galones`) is optional, but without it KilomeTracker cannot calculate fuel efficiency metrics like km/liter or cost/km.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your vehicle dashboard and stats">
    Return to the **Dashboard** to see your vehicle card with updated mileage. Click the **Stats** button on your vehicle card to open the full statistics page, which shows:

    - Total routes, refuels, maintenance records, and expenses
    - Cost breakdown by category (fuel, maintenance, other)
    - Fuel efficiency metrics (km/liter, km/gallon, cost/km)
    - Total cost of ownership
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  KilomeTracker is mobile-first — the experience is optimized for smartphones. Filters open in a bottom sheet, and history pages show cards on mobile and tables on desktop.
</Note>
