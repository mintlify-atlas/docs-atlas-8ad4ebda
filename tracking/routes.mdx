title: "Routes & mileage"
description: "Log trips, track distances, and monitor total mileage per vehicle."
---

Every trip you log is stored as a route record tied to a specific vehicle. KilomeTracker accumulates those distances automatically so your total mileage is always up to date.

## Logging a route

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Route form">
    From the sidebar, click **Add Route**. You can also reach it from the dashboard by selecting a vehicle and clicking **Add Route**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a vehicle">
    Choose the vehicle alias from the dropdown. Only active vehicles appear in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the distance">
    Type the distance covered in the **Distance (km)** field (`distanciaRecorrida`). Use the actual kilometers driven for this trip — not the odometer reading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date">
    Pick the trip date (`fecha`) using the date picker. It defaults to today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes (optional)">
    Use the **Additional notes** field (`notasAdicionales`) to record anything relevant — route description, purpose, passengers, or road conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the route">
    Click **Save Route**. The record is created immediately and your vehicle's `kilometrajeTotal` is updated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Routes history

The **Routes History** page lists every route across all your vehicles. You can filter the list to find specific records quickly.

### Filtering routes

| Filter | Field | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Vehicle | `vehicleAlias` | Show routes for one specific vehicle only. |
| Start date | `startDate` | Include routes logged on or after this date. |
| End date | `endDate` | Include routes logged on or before this date. |

On mobile, tap **Filters** to open the filter panel from the bottom of the screen. On desktop, the filter panel appears as a card above the results table.

### Mobile vs. desktop views

- **Mobile**: each route appears as a card showing the vehicle alias, distance, date, and any notes.
- **Desktop**: routes are displayed in a full-width table with sortable columns.

## Mileage accumulation

Every saved route automatically adds `distanciaRecorrida` to the vehicle's `kilometrajeTotal`. You do not need to update the odometer manually. The running total is visible on the vehicle card in the dashboard and on the **Vehicle Statistics** page.

<Tip>
  Log routes as soon as possible after each trip so your mileage total stays accurate and your fuel efficiency calculations reflect real driving patterns.
</Tip>

## Editing a route

To correct a distance or date, open **Routes History**, find the route, and click the edit icon. All fields can be updated after creation.

## Deleting a route

<Warning>
  Route deletion is permanent. There is no soft delete or undo — once a route is deleted, the record is gone and the mileage is not automatically subtracted from your vehicle's `kilometrajeTotal`.
</Warning>

To delete a route, open **Routes History** and click the delete icon on the route card (mobile) or table row (desktop). Confirm the action in the dialog.
