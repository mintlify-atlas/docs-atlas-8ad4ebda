## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [kilometracker.vercel.app](https://kilometracker.vercel.app) and register with your email and a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first vehicle">
    From the dashboard, click **Add Vehicle** and enter your vehicle's alias, brand, model year, license plates, and initial mileage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your first trip">
    Navigate to **Add Route**, select your vehicle, enter the distance and date, and save. Your total mileage updates immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your first refuel">
    Go to **Add Refuel**, choose your vehicle, enter the fuel type, amount spent, and gallons filled. KilomeTracker calculates your efficiency automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
